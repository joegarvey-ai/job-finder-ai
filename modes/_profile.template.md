### Example 1 — Financial Analyst targeting FP&A leadership

| Archetype | Themes | Buyer value |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| FP&A Manager / Senior FP&A | Annual planning, forecast accuracy, budget vs actual, partner-with-business | A finance partner who turns numbers into operational decisions |
| Corporate Development Analyst | M&A diligence, integration modeling, market sizing | A modeler who builds defensible cases for capital allocation |
| Strategic Finance | Three-statement modeling, scenario planning, investor reporting | A storyteller who connects financial performance to strategy |

Avoid: Pure accounting / month-end close roles, audit roles.

### Example 2 — Senior Designer targeting design leadership

| Archetype | Themes | Buyer value |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Design Systems Lead | Component libraries, tokens, cross-product consistency, design ops | A designer who makes 50 designers ship like one team |
| Senior Product Designer (IC) | End-to-end product flows, research-to-ship, design partnerships | A designer who owns full features and ships measurable outcomes |
| Head of UX Research | Research practice, generative+evaluative methods, insight to roadmap | A research leader who shifts product decisions with evidence |

Avoid: Visual-design-only roles (no product/interaction scope), marketing creative direction.
