Director of AI Product

Acme AI is hiring a Director of AI Product to lead the strategy and roadmap for our LLM platform. You will own the end-to-end product lifecycle for our developer-facing AI tools used by 2M+ developers worldwide.

What you'll do:
- Define and own the multi-year product roadmap for our LLM/RAG platform and developer tooling
- Lead a cross-functional team of PMs, engineers, and designers (15+ direct and matrixed reports)
- Partner with engineering leadership on technical strategy, buy-vs-build decisions, and evals
- Drive go-to-market alignment with sales, marketing, and customer success
- Define and track product KPIs: adoption, engagement, retention, NPS

Required:
- 8+ years of product management experience, 3+ at Director level or above
- Track record shipping production AI/ML or developer platform products at scale (millions of users)
- Strong strategic thinking and ability to operate at the intersection of business and technical strategy
- Experience leading cross-functional teams of 10+
- Excellent written and verbal communication

Nice to have:
- Experience in B2B SaaS or developer tools
- Background in machine learning, LLMs, or AI/agentic systems
- MBA or equivalent

Compensation:
- Base: $250K–$300K
- Total: $400K–$550K (base + equity + bonus)
- Remote-friendly within US
- Full benefits, 401k match, unlimited PTO

Location: Remote (US) or hybrid in San Francisco
